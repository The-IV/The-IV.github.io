FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the app:


Q: Why don't my earnings and/or expenses display in my summary chart?
A: Ensure that you have entered at least one tip/expense. The summary chart requires data from at least one category to display a financial breakdown. (Also be sure the correct month and year have been selected from the top of the summary screen)

Q: Can I reset my data?
A: Yes, click the three horizontal lines located in the top left corner of the app and select "Reset Data" from the dropdown menu.

Q: Is my data stored securely?
A: Yes, all data is stored locally on your device and is not shared with any third parties.

Q: Can I export my data?
A: Not currently, but this feature will be included in a future update.

Q: Will there be an Android version?
A: Yes, I am currently working on an Android version, though I am unsure of the exact release date

Q: How can I provide feedback or suggest features?
A: You can contact me via the email towards the top of the page.

Troubleshooting

If you are experiencing issues with the app, please try the following steps:


- Ensure you are using the latest version of the app.
- Restart your device.
- Check for any available updates for your iOS.
