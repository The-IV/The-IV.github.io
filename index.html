About Me

I’m Floyd — a recent Computer Science graduate from the University of Houston (Fall 2024) with a passion for building thoughtful, user-focused software. I'm currently pursuing opportunities in software development, with hands-on experience in Python, C++, C#, and Swift. My skill set also includes front-end web development, using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build and design responsive, clean interfaces — like the site you're visiting right now. Outside of coursework, I’ve applied my development skills to real-world projects, including a solo-developed finance app launching soon on the Apple App Store. It's designed to help service workers manage their finances more effectively. After working in the service industry for over a year, I understand firsthand the unique challenges of budgeting with an inconsistent income. In addition to coding, I produce music and engineer artists, blending creativity and technical problem-solving across mediums. Whether I’m refining a piece of code or a mix, I bring the same attention to detail and drive to improve. Thanks for stopping by — feel free to reach out if you'd like to collaborate, chat, or learn more about what I'm working on.
