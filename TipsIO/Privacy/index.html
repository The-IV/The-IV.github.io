Privacy Policy — TipTrack

Effective date: September 17, 2025

Overview

TipTrack (“the App”) is a lightweight personal finance and earnings tracker designed to store your income and expense information locally on your device. TipTrack does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data to us or to third parties.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information, analytics, advertising data, or usage telemetry. All data you enter (earnings, expenses, categories, settings, etc.) remains stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or remote data storage. No user data is shared with any external services.

Local Storage

All records and settings are saved to your device’s local storage. Because data is local, you remain in full control — deleting the app will remove the locally stored data. If you want to back up your data, please do so using standard device-level backup tools.

Security

While TipTrack keeps data on-device, you should follow standard device security practices (passcode, device encryption, iCloud backup settings) to protect your information.

Changes to This Policy

If the app’s data practices change in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated. We will indicate the new effective date at the top of this page.
